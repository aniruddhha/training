  3. Enrollment Guide

Enroll with me to embark on a transformative learning journey that aligns with your goals and aspirations in the field of technology and programming.

Demo Lecture:

Experience our training methodology firsthand through 2 complimentary demo lectures. Alternatively, you can explore our instructional videos on YouTube to get a preview of our teaching approach.

Payment Process:

For fee payment, ensure you receive payment details only from an authorized representative of Mr. Aniruddha Kudalkar. Follow the instructions provided by the representative. Please note that, in accordance with government regulations, a GST rate of 18% will be applied to the total fees.

Enrollment Process:

Enroll in your desired course or bootcamp by sharing your contact details – name, mobile number, email, city, and educational qualifications – with an authorized representative of Mr. Aniruddha Kudalkar. They will guide you through the enrollment procedure.

Privacy Policies Acceptance:

By enrolling in any of our courses or bootcamps, you signify your acceptance of our privacy policies. Kindly review the policies to understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Online Training Setup:

Ensure you have access to the necessary hardware, software, and a stable internet connection for an effective online learning experience. A modern computer, microphone, webcam, and relevant software installations may be required based on the course requirements.

Confirmation of Enrollment:

Once your enrollment is confirmed and fees are paid as per the authorized representative's instructions, you will receive official communication from Mr. Aniruddha Kudalkar Training Institute confirming your participation.

Course/Bootcamp Details:

Subsequent to enrollment, you will receive comprehensive information regarding the course/bootcamp schedule, curriculum, learning resources, and any prerequisites.

Stay Updated:

Keep an eye on your provided contact channels for important updates and communications regarding your selected course/bootcamp.

Support:

For any queries or clarifications during the enrollment process, feel free to reach out to our authorized representatives. They are here to assist you and provide the necessary guidance.

My VISON is to provide research-driven training, MISSION is to help people achieve financial freedom via my courses & VALUES revolve around Coding, Technology & Research.
